Homemade Mexican Food

Welcome, welcome. Are you tired of overpriced Mexican food? Or, would you love to learn how to prepare it? If you are, this place is going to help you get delicious food without making your wallet cry. Here you can buy authentic Mexican food or learn how to cook it. Our mission is to bring amazing flavors to your table on a budget or to help you learn basic recipes to have your own meal at home. Please sign-up for classes or buy any of our menu items by clicking in our navigation bar the option you desire.

What type of food you are going to find here? Our team is conformed by a mix of people from different Mexican regions, but as we all lived in Baja California previously, we are bringing a northern style on our recipes. We are please to serve our community, especially our loved students trying to reach out to their dreams.
